Welcome to Castle Defense Idle!

Targeting

In this idle game you have auto-targeting and click to target capabilities! This game functions best in full-screen and on a PC/laptop, so if you're in full-screen you are able to click on the monsters to target. Otherwise, you can click on the monster's name to target. You will be able to see your current target in the middle of the player menu on the screen.

Special Abilities

Your royal sprites have two special abilities. Each has a timer when it can be used again, represented by a yellow bar. When the ability is ready, the bar flashes red and yellow! There is an all-in attack where your team massively damages all foes, and a group heal which regains a precentage of health points lost.
